Learn more about the Genre of the Anime you have heard about.

Shonen

Genre of Anime that is focused on a main character. Mainly based on action,adventure,fighting. Some examples are One Piece, Naruto, Attack on Titan, Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood, DBZ etc.

Sports

Sports manga is a genre of Japanese manga and anime that focuses on stories involving sports and other athletic and competitive pursuits. Famous sports anime: Haikyuu, Kuroko no Basket, Hajime no Ippo

Slice of Life

Slice of Life anime generally revolves around the daily life of the main character and the people involved in their Life. It is usually set in a school background. Few notable SOL anime to mention: Your Lie in April, Silent Voice, Clannad.
