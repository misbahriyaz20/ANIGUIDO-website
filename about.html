Misbah and Jayanth are CSE students of BMS College of Engineering currently studying in 2nd year. Both of them watch anime and are passionate about it, they have created this site with a vision to bring anime into other's lives too and create an impact in their lives just like it did for them. They want to share awareness about this amazing art form to the rest who are not aware of it or neglect it for many reasons. This site will guide you from "what is anime" through "where to start from", and we assure you after you give it a shot, you will fall in awe with it.

Made by Misbah Riyaz and Jayanth.
